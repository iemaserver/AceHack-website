Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Browse through our FAQ to find everything you need to know about AceHack 5.0

What is AceHack 5.0? +

AceHack 5.0 is a 36-hour hackathon where students and innovators create real-world tech solutions. It's a platform to learn, build, network, and compete for amazing prizes while working on projects that matter.

When and where is AceHack 5.0 taking place? +

AceHack 5.0 will be held on March 7-8th, 2026. The event location will be announced soon. Stay tuned to our social media channels for updates!

Who can participate in AceHack 5.0? +

AceHack 5.0 is open to all students currently enrolled in any educational institution across India. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, everyone is welcome!

How do I register for the hackathon? +

Click the "Register Now" button on our homepage and fill out the registration form with your details. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is complete.

Is there a registration fee? +

No! AceHack 5.0 is completely free to participate in. We believe in making innovation accessible to everyone.

Can I participate individually or do I need a team? +

You can participate either individually or in teams of up to 4 members. We recommend forming teams to enhance collaboration and learning!

When is the registration deadline? +

When we reach maximum capacity the registrations will be closed. We recommend registering early to secure your spot!

Can I change my team after registering? +

Yes, team changes are allowed up to March 1st 2026. Please contact us at discord and raise a ticket for modifications.

What should I bring to the hackathon? +

Bring your laptop, chargers, student ID, and enthusiasm! We'll provide food, drinks, WiFi, and workspace. We will provide accomodation. Hacker Guide will be shared soon.

Will food and accommodation be provided? +

Yes! We'll provide all meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the 36-hour event. Accommodation arrangements will be shared closer to the event date.

What are the hackathon tracks? +

We have 8 tracks: Blockchain & Crypto, Web & App Dev, IOT, AR & VR, Game Dev, Data & Gen AI, Cloud & DevOps, Cyber Security and Open Innovation. Choose the track that aligns with your project idea!

Will there be mentors available during the event? +

Absolutely! We have 20+ mentors from various tech domains who will be available throughout the hackathon to guide you, answer questions, and help debug your projects.

Are there any workshops or tech talks? +

Yes! We'll have workshops, tech talks, and sessions by industry experts throughout the event. Schedule will be shared with registered participants.

What technologies can we use? +

You're free to use any programming languages, frameworks, APIs, or platforms you prefer. Choose what works best for your project idea!

Can we start working on our project before the hackathon? +

No, all code must be written during the 36-hour event period. However, you can come with an idea, research, and design mockups prepared beforehand.

Do we need to have a working prototype? +

While a working prototype is preferred, we also value innovative ideas and solid execution plans. Focus on demonstrating your concept effectively!

Will internet and power outlets be available? +

Yes! High-speed WiFi and charging stations will be available throughout the venue. We'll ensure you have all the infrastructure needed to build great projects. Due to the venue size and number of participants, we recommend bringing your own power bank and personal hotspot as a backup.

Can we use open-source libraries and APIs? +

Yes! You're encouraged to use open-source libraries, APIs, and frameworks. Just make sure to properly attribute any third-party code you use.

What is the total prize pool? +

The total prize pool will be revealed soon! This includes prizes for top 3 teams, best beginner team, and all-girls team categories.

How will projects be judged? +

Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, technical complexity, design & usability, real-world impact, and presentation quality by our expert panel of judges.

When will winners be announced? +

Winners will be announced during the closing ceremony on March 8, 2026. All participants must be present for the final presentations and ceremony.

Will I get a certificate of participation? +

Yes! All participants will receive a certificate of participation. Winners and special category awardees will receive additional recognition certificates.

Are there any sponsor prizes or tracks? +

Yes! Our sponsors may offer additional prizes and special tracks. Details will be announced to registered participants before the event.

What if I'm a beginner with no prior hackathon experience? +

Perfect! AceHack 5.0 welcomes beginners. We have a special "Best Beginner" category and mentors dedicated to helping first-timers. It's a great place to learn!

Is there a code of conduct? +

Yes, all participants must follow MLH code of conduct. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated. MLH Code of Conduct: https://github.com/MLH/mlh-policies/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md

What if I can't find a team? +

Don't worry! We have a team formation group in our discord server where you can meet other participants and form teams based on interests and skills.

Can we work on hardware projects? +

Absolutely! Hardware projects are welcome, especially in the IoT track. Please bring your own hardware components as. We will try to fulfill any other requirements as available.

How long is the presentation/demo time? +

There is no stage presentation in Acehack, Judges will visit your alloted room during presentation. Each team will have 3 minutes to present their project followed by 2 minutes of Q&A with judges. Make your demo impactful and concise!

Can participants from other cities attend? +

Yes! AceHack 5.0 is open to students from across India. We encourage diversity and welcome participants from all regions.
